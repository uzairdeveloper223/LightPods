Overview

LightPods is a free, open-source Android application developed by Uzair Mughal. This app is designed to provide battery monitoring, ear detection, and connection management for AirPods-compatible Bluetooth earbuds.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Do NOT Collect

LightPods does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or telemetry.
  • No crash reporting services.
  • No advertising frameworks.
  • No user accounts or authentication.
  • No cloud servers or network requests.
  • No location data is recorded or transmitted.

Permissions Used

The app requires certain Android permissions to function. All data accessed by these permissions stays entirely on your device.

  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT / BLUETOOTH_SCAN - Required to detect and communicate with your Bluetooth earbuds.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION - Required by Android versions before Android 12 for BLE scanning. We do not read or store your GPS location.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE - Used to run a lightweight background monitor that keeps battery info up to date.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW - Optional. Shows a connection popup over other apps when pods are detected.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS - Optional. Prevents Android from killing the background service.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS - Shows battery status in the notification bar.

Bluetooth Data

The app reads BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) advertisements broadcast by your earbuds. This data includes:

  • Battery levels for each pod and the case.
  • Ear detection status.
  • Microphone assignment.
  • Case lid state.
  • Device MAC address.
  • Signal strength (RSSI).

This data is processed in real time on your device and is never stored permanently, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

LightPods uses zero third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data. The app is completely offline and self-contained.

Open Source

The complete source code is publicly available at github.com/uzairdeveloper223/lightpods. You can audit every line of code to verify these claims.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since the app collects no data, changes are expected to be rare.
