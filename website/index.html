LightPods

A Material Design 3 companion for AirPods-compatible earbuds. It shows per-pod battery, ear detection, microphone status, and case state from real BLE proximity data.

widgets App capabilities

Real earbud state, not generic Bluetooth guesses.

LightPods is built around the data your earbuds already broadcast, then presents it with Material You surfaces that feel native on modern Android.

battery_full

Per-pod battery

Left pod, right pod, and case levels are decoded from Apple proximity pairing nibbles.

mic

Microphone tracking

Primary pod status is parsed from the BLE status byte and mirrored in the Android UI.

hearing

Ear detection

In-ear and case state are shown as live app rows, notifications, and popup context.

notifications_active

System overlay

A connection popup appears when pods are nearby, with dismiss behavior that avoids repeated spam.

speed

Background monitor

A lightweight foreground service keeps battery status fresh while the main app is closed.

palette

Dynamic color

Android 12+ dynamic color, light and dark themes, and Material 3 component spacing.

memory Under the hood

Decoded protocol details.

Based on Apple Continuity Protocol reverse engineering and btmon packet analysis.

BLE type 0x07

Proximity pairing.

Company ID 0x004C

Apple, Inc.

Battery 4-bit nibbles

0-10 = 0-100%, 15 = N/A.

Fallback AT+IPHONEACCEV

(value + 1) x 10.

Microphone bit 5 XOR

Status byte flip detection.

Chipset Qualcomm QCC

BR/EDR and BLE dual mode.

Free, open source, and offline by design.

No analytics, crash reporters, advertising SDKs, user accounts, cloud servers, or network telemetry. Bluetooth data stays on your device.
